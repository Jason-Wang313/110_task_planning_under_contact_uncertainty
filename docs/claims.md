# Claims

## Supported Locally

- Branch-level contact-outcome belief is a useful abstraction for task planning under uncertain future contact outcomes.
- `contact_belief_branch_audit_v5` beats the strongest non-oracle baseline, `proposed_contact_belief_tamp_v4`, on held-out hard success and utility.
- The v5 method improves belief ECE, invalid precondition rate, recovery success, damage, wasted actions, and irreversible commitment relative to the strongest non-oracle baseline.
- Paired hard-split utility wins are 10/10 seeds against the strongest non-oracle baseline.
- The mechanism survives ablations, a maximum-stress endpoint, and a nontrivial strict fixed-risk endpoint.
- Bright boxed citation links and generated evidence tables are present in the 25-page PDF.

## Not Claimed

- ICLR main readiness.
- Real robot readiness.
- Hardware safety certification.
- Superiority over all POMDP, robust planning, or contact-implicit systems.
- A trained robot foundation-policy integration.
- External benchmark generalization.

## Required Before Submission

- Real robot or accepted high-fidelity benchmark validation.
- External trained policy/planner baselines.
- Force, tactile, proprioceptive, or contact-label traces.
- Rollout videos and deployment logs.
- Pre-registered external terminal gates.
