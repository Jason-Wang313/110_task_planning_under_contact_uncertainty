# Literature Map

## Task And Motion Planning

TAMP already combines symbolic plans with continuous feasibility. The paper must not claim that symbolic-geometric integration is new.

## Planning Under Uncertainty

POMDPs, contingent planning, robust MPC, and risk-aware planning already maintain or hedge uncertainty. The paper's contribution must be contact-specific.

## Contact-Rich Manipulation

Contact uncertainty, jamming, slip, compliance, and release failures are known challenges. The paper contributes a local planning audit, not a full hardware solution.

## Narrow Contribution

The defensible claim is that contact-outcome beliefs, recovery affordance branches, diagnostic contact probes, chance constraints, and damage-aware scoring improve local task planning under uncertain future contact.
